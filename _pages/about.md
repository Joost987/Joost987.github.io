Hi!I am Joost de Nijs, a double Master of Science student in Applied Mathematics and Applied Physics at Delft University. I am currently finishing my Master's thesis under supervision of Bas Janssens, Yaroslav Blanter and Koenraad Schalm. It is titled: *Asymptotic symmetries of three dimensional Anti-de-Sitter space from a perspective of representation theory*. As the title mentions, it is about the asymptotic symmetries of three dimensional Anti-de-Sitter (AdS) space. These were first discovered by J.D. Brown and Marc Hennaux in 1986, in their article: [Central charges in the canonical realization of asymptotic symmetries: An example from three dimensional gravity](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/BF01211590). However, we give a slightly different definition of the asymptotic symmetry algebra, which is more oriented towards quantum mechanics in a sense. This change in definition gives you a slightly different asymptotic symmetry algebra, which is closely related to the symmetry algebra of 2D CFT's. For more details, you'll have to wait until my thesis is finished (my defense is on June 17th), or you can send me an email.
